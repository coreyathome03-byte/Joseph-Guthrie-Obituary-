In Loving Memory of Joseph Guthrie

1997 – 2025 | Inuvik, Northwest Territories

Obituary

Joseph Guthrie, aged 28, of Inuvik, Northwest Territories, passed away peacefully in 2025.

Born and raised in Inuvik, Joseph was known for his kind heart, easygoing spirit, and deep love for the North. He had a quiet strength and a thoughtful way of caring for those around him. Whether helping friends, spending time with family, or taking in the peaceful beauty of the land, Joseph found meaning in simple moments and genuine connections.

He will be deeply missed by his family, friends, and all who knew him. Joseph’s memory will live on through the stories, laughter, and love he shared with so many during his short but meaningful life.

Memorial Service

A memorial gathering will be held in Inuvik to celebrate Joseph’s life, with details to be shared by his family.

May he rest peacefully, surrounded by the northern lights he loved so much.
